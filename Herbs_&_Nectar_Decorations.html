Herbs & Nectar Decorations

Herbs & Nectar is a Decoration theme added alongside the release of Honeymoon Cookie.
It is based off the Sanctum of Blight and contains the Cookie Decors of Cloudy Tonic Cookie and Honeymoon Cookie.
Owning at least one of each of the 15 decorations rewards 300 Crystals.


The content displayed in this page is in conceptual stages and more will be updated in the future, alongside additional interactive features.

This decoration seems to be a Higashi, a type of Wagashi (traditional Japanese confection) made out of fine grained Wasanbon Sugar and shaped into a Nelumbo nucifera (also known as Pink Lotus, Sacred Lotus, Indian Lotus).
